About us

The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world.

The owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons.

The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company.

Our goal is to promote and provide the specialized services and meet the needs of temple patrons who come to serve in the temple or who participate in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips..

The Temple Inn & Suites reflects the Church culture and support a temple oriented theme as well as the concept of being a haven of comfort and accessibility.
